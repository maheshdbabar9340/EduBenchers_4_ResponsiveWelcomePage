India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia.

  • Mumbai

    Mumbai (formerly called Bombay) is a densely populated city on India’s west coast. A financial center, it's India's largest city.

  • Chennai

    Chennai, on the Bay of Bengal in eastern India, is the capital of the state of Tamil Nadu. Home to the Tamil film industry, Chennai is also known as a major film production centre.

  • Bangalore

    Bengaluru (also called Bangalore) is the capital of India's southern Karnataka state. The center of India's high-tech industry, the city is also known for its parks and nightlife.
